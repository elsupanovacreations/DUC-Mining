About DUC Mining

The Down Under Consortium (DUC Mining and Explorations) is a dynamic Botswana-Australian start-up dedicated to the discovery, extraction, and ethical trade of rare earth minerals, with a primary focus on diamonds.

Our operations are centered on delivering sustainable and conflict-free mining practices in Botswana, a country globally renowned for its diamond-rich heritage.

Exploration Highlights

Discovering the potential of Botswana's most prolific diamond regions

Khumo Gaufi A (KG A)

Preliminary geological surveys indicate rich deposits of high-quality diamonds, with early core sampling suggesting the potential for significant discoveries.

"Khumo Gaufi" means "Wealth in Reach" in Setswana

Khumo Gaufi B (KG B)

Exhibiting similar geological characteristics, KG B is expected to complement KG A in yielding large, high-purity diamonds.

Located in the Orapa/Letlhakane Kimberlite District near Karowe Diamond Mine
